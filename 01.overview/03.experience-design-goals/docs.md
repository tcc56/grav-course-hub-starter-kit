Key experience design goals for students:
* Engaging
* Organized
* Relevant
* Convenient
* Enjoyable

Key experience design goals for facilitator(s)
* Controllable (i.e. manageable)
* Pliable (i.e. flexible)
* Efficient
* Enjoyable (hey, instructors are people too...)
